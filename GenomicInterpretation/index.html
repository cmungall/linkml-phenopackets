    Class: GenomicInterpretation

    A statement about the contribution of a genomic element towards the observed phenotype. Note that this does not intend to encode any knowledge or results of specific computations.

    URI: interpretation:GenomicInterpretation

    Slots

    Name Range Cardinality Description Info
    gene GeneDescriptor 0..1 .
    interpretationStatus InterpretationStatus 0..1 .
    subjectOrBiosampleId xsd:string 0..1 identifier for the subject of the interpretation. This MUST be the individual id or a biosample id of the enclosing phenopacket. .
    variantInterpretation VariantInterpretation 0..1 .
